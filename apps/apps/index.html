Ludo - A strategy board game ⬇️

Ludo is a strategy board game for two to four players, in which the players race their four tokens from start to finish according to the rolls of a single die. Users can also play with the device. The game is available on iOS and macOS.

Minesweeper 95 ⬇️

Minesweeper is a single-player puzzle video game. The objective of the game is to clear a rectangular board containing hidden "mines" or bombs without detonating any of them, with help from clues about the number of neighboring mines in each field. The game is available on iOS and macOS. Players can compete with other players and ranked in various leadership boards available in App.
